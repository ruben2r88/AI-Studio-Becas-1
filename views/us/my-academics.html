Financial Aid & Scholarships

Some students may qualify for financial aid or scholarships through Missouri State University. Eligibility depends on enrollment status and academic program.

Scholarships
  • Most academic scholarships require full-time enrollment.
  • Some scholarships may have limitations when living off campus.
  • Availability and requirements depend on MSU policies.
Student Loans
  • Student loans usually require half-time or full-time enrollment.
  • Loans are typically available only to degree-seeking students.
  • Requirements vary depending on your country and program.
Maintain enrollment status

To remain eligible for financial aid or loans, students must maintain the required enrollment status each semester, according to MSU rules.

Exact conditions depend on Missouri State University policies. Always talk to your Eture coach before making financial decisions.
